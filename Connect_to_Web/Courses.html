Our Courses

What we give

Horizon University offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in diverse fields of study. Our undergraduate programs include majors in business, engineering, arts and humanities, science, and more. Our graduate programs offer specialized studies in various fields such as law, medicine, education, and many more. Our courses are taught by experienced faculty who are dedicated to providing students with hands-on learning experiences. The coursework is designed to challenge students and prepare them for the workforce and real-world applications.

Computer Science

Learn the latest programming languages and technologies to design and develop software applications.

Business Administration

Develop your skills in areas such as finance, marketing, and management to become a successful business leader.

Environmental Science

Understand the impact of human activities on the environment and learn to develop sustainable solutions to address environmental challenges.
